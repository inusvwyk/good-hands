## Bidding Strategy

**Phase 1 (Days 1–30):** Maximize Clicks, Max CPC cap R8.00. At R150/day and R2.94 avg CPC expect ~50 clicks/day.
**Phase 2 (After 30 conversions):** Switch to Target CPA. Set initial target at observed cost-per-lead from Phase 1.
**Phase 3 (Scale):** Increase budget 20% at a time once tCPA is stable for 14 days.
